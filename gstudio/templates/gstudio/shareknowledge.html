Mahatma Gandhi and the Indian National Movement

Activity 2 for students
  1. Engage the students in the given pictures and ask them to critically express their views on each slide.
  2. Ask students to visit website nroer.gov.in and to make a presentation on gandhi’s role in the Indian national movement.
  3. Discuss the Gandhian principles of truth, non-violence and satyagraha and ask students to write an essay on the relevance of these principles in present society.

Mahatma Gandhi’s life in South Africa

Activity 1 for students
  1. Engage the students in the given pictures, and:
    • Ask them to write at least five points on each picture.
    • Ask them to critically express their views on each slide.
  2. Explore Gandhi’s life with the help of given timeline and ask students to prepare a report on “What led the turning point in gandhi’s life” ?
  3. Discuss the given map of South Africa (1900 C.) and compare it with that of now.

Mahatma Gandhi and the social welfare activities

Activity 3 for students
  1. Engage the students in the given pictures and ask them to critically express their views on each slide.
  2. Discuss the scenario of the society during 19th centaury in india with students and ask them :
    • To examine what led Gandhiji to run social welfare activities parallel to Indian national movement.
    • To prepare a report on the role of women in Indian national movement.
    • To prepare a report on Gandhi’s untouchability removal campaign.
